PRIVACY POLICY

HeartPrayerMusic.com
Effective Date: 12 February 2026


At Heart Prayer Music, your privacy is deeply important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website and services. By using our services, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information to provide our custom musical services, including:

  • ■ Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and billing address
  • ■ Payment Information: Payment details (processed securely through third-party payment processors; we do not store your full credit card numbers)
  • ■ Account Information (optional): Username, password, preferences, and settings if you create an account
  • ■ User-Generated Content: Personal stories, song requests, feedback, or testimonial videos that you provide
  • ■ Device and Usage Information: IP address, browser type, device information, pages you visit, and analytics data to improve our services
  • ■ Cookies & Similar Technologies: We use cookies and analytics to enhance your website experience. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.


2. How We Use Your Information

  • ■ Fulfill orders: create, deliver, and manage your custom songs
  • ■ Communicate with you: order updates, service changes, or responses to inquiries
  • ■ Improve our services: website analytics, testing, and user experience
  • ■ Marketing (with consent): newsletters, promotions, or social media posts featuring testimonials you approve

We may also use your information to comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or unauthorized activity.



3. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

We respect your privacy. We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • ■ Service providers: payment processors, hosting, IT support, shipping, and analytics
  • ■ Business partners: marketing or advertising partners, but only with your consent where required by law
  • ■ Legal or safety reasons: to comply with laws, respond to legal requests, enforce our Terms, or protect rights

Your interactions with third-party links, widgets, or platforms are subject to their privacy policies.



4. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • ■ Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • ■ Receive a copy of your personal data in a portable format
  • ■ Opt out of marketing communications
  • ■ Withdraw consent for previously approved uses of your data
  • ■ Restrict or object to certain types of processing

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@heartprayermusic.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing requests.



5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • ■ Provide our services
  • ■ Comply with legal obligations
  • ■ Resolve disputes
  • ■ Enforce agreements

Once your information is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.



6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption of data in transit, restricted access controls, and regular security audits. However, no system is completely secure. Please take care to protect your account credentials and sensitive information.



7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction). In regions such as the European Union where the age threshold is higher (e.g., 16), we apply the stricter standard. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us to have it deleted.



8. International Transfers

Your information may be stored and processed outside your home country. If we transfer data internationally, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with applicable law, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by regulators or other recognized legal mechanisms.



9. Changes to This Policy

We use cookies and analytics to enhance your website experience. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings. In addition, where required by law, we provide a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept, reject, or customize which categories of cookies are used (e.g., essential, analytics, marketing).
